The Visionary

The leader who goes the way & shows the way
MRITZ is the brainchild of Mr. Mashood, our Founder and Chairman. The company is truly the culmination of twenty-three years of research, experience and understanding of Mr. Mashood. His vision was to form a company that serves as a hub for innovation—a place where technology and human intelligence  co-exist seamlessly. 
Powered by his vision and willingness to move ahead and innovate, he formed a team of bright minds. MRITZ functions under his mentorship and foresight.
